# E-Commerce Executive Performance Dashboard (Google Analytics 360)

## Project Overview
This project focuses on transforming raw, nested Google Analytics 360 data into a high-level executive dashboard. By leveraging BigQuery for data engineering and Looker Studio for visualization, this project identifies critical disparities between traffic volume and revenue value across different user segments and device categories.

## Tools & Technologies
- **Data Warehouse:** Google BigQuery
- **Query Language:** Standard SQL (Common Table Expressions, Unnesting)
- **Business Intelligence:** Looker Studio
- **Data Cleaning:** Calculated Fields & Regex Standardization

## Key Business Insights & Storytelling

<details>
<summary><b>View 1: Global Business Health (Unfiltered)</b></summary>

### The Narrative
In February 2017, the e-commerce platform maintained a stable "pulse" with **$108.8K in Revenue** and a **1.27% Conversion Rate**. While the acquisition funnel is diverse, the business relies heavily on brand equity rather than paid discovery.

- **Revenue Growth:** Achieved an **11.1% increase** compared to the previous period.
- **Acquisition Efficiency:** **Direct Traffic** is the primary driver, accounting for **42.1%** of total users.
- **Technical Implementation:** Engineered custom `CASE` statements to clean raw system labels (`(none)`, `(not set)`) into user-friendly business categories.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>View 2: Desktop Analysis (The Profit Engine)</b></summary>

### The Narrative
Filtering the data by **Desktop** reveals the core financial engine of the company. Desktop users are "High-Intent" shoppers who complete the majority of the business transactions.

- **Superior Conversion:** The conversion rate climbs to **1.63%** when isolated to Desktop users.
- **Revenue Concentration:** Desktop accounts for **$105.1K**, representing over **96%** of total monthly revenue.
- **The Loyalty Factor:** **84.9%** of Desktop revenue is generated by **Direct** visitors, indicating a highly loyal customer base that skips search engines to shop directly.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>View 3: Mobile Analysis (The Growth Opportunity)</b></summary>

### The Narrative
The **Mobile** segment reveals a significant "Conversion Gap." While social media and search successfully drive traffic, the mobile environment currently acts as a "Discovery" phase rather than a "Closing" phase.

- **Performance Friction:** Mobile conversion rate drops to **0.43%**, nearly 4x lower than Desktop.
- **The Facebook Signal:** Social acquisition (**m.facebook.com**) is a top-5 traffic driver on mobile, highlighting the success of mobile-first marketing.
- **Strategic Recommendation:** Identified a 46% revenue drop in this segment, suggesting a need for mobile-UI optimization and streamlined checkout processes.
</details>

## Project Structure
- `01_SQL_Queries/`: BigQuery scripts used to unnest and aggregate GA360 data.
- `02_Visuals/`: PDF exports and high-resolution screenshots of the dashboard.
- `03_Cleaned_Data/`: Sample CSV exports of the cleaned "Medium" categories.

## Conclusion
By implementing cross-filtering and automated data cleaning, this dashboard provides a 360-degree view of the e-commerce funnel. It proves that while the business has strong brand loyalty, there is significant untapped revenue potential in the mobile user experience.
