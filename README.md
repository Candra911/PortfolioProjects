## 🚀 Featured Project: The Great Tech Reset — AI Edition (Python)

**Background Story**
Between 2024 and 2026, the tech industry entered a new phase of restructuring. Moving beyond the "Post-Pandemic" cost-cutting of 2023, I investigated a more profound shift: **The AI Substitution Effect**. Using Python, I tracked how companies began reallocating billions in human capital budgets toward AI infrastructure, marking a fundamental "Reset" of the global workforce.

**Key Advanced Insights**
* **The Pivot Point:** Identified February 2026 as the historic "Cross-over," where AI-driven layoffs (194,000+) surpassed traditional layoffs for the first time.
* **Strategic Substitution:** Proved that **Amazon** and **Google** led the shift, with over 2 million layoffs explicitly tied to "AI-First" strategies rather than simple cost-cutting.
* **Regional Intensity:** Discovered that while the US has the highest volume, **Singapore** has a 46% AI-intensity rate, showing that high-tech hubs are automating faster than large nations.
