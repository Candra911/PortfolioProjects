## Featured Projects

### [The Great Tech Reset: Layoff Analysis](./The-Great-Tech-Reset/)
**Tools:** SQL, Data Storytelling, Executive Reporting  
A deep-dive investigation into 2,000+ global tech layoffs between 2020 and 2023. This project analyzes the correlation between funding levels and company survival rates.

* **Key Insight:** January 2023 saw **84,714** layoffs—a 300% increase over the initial pandemic shock in March 2020.
* **Technical Highlight:** Identified "Big Players" like **Quibi ($1.8B)** and **BlockFi ($1B)** that collapsed despite massive funding.
* **Efficiency Metric:** Discovered that **Low Funding (<$50M)** startups averaged a 34.5% workforce reduction.

### [Aviation Financial Resilience Analysis](./Aviation-Financial-Impact/)
**Tools:** Advanced Excel, Power Query, Dynamic Dashboarding  
An interactive dashboard tracking global airline profitability across 8 distinct conflict phases, focusing on fuel price volatility and risk mitigation strategies.

* **Key Insight:** Visualized the "V-shaped" recovery of the aviation industry from the **COVID-19 Collapse** through to **Stabilisation**.
* **Technical Highlight:** Built a 3-pillar dashboard using **Secondary Axes** to compare Passenger Volume against Profit Margins and Fuel Cost percentages.
