<summary><b>The Great Tech Reset: Layoff Analysis (SQL)</b></summary>

### Background Story
Between 2020 and 2021, the tech industry over-hired during a "cheap money" era. When the economy shifted in 2022, I used SQL to move past the headlines and analyze the cold, hard data of the 2023 "Tech Winter".

### Key Advanced Insights
* **Lagging Correction:** Discovered a **300% surge** in layoffs in January 2023 (84,700+ jobs) compared to the initial 2020 pandemic shock.
* **The Scale of Impact:** While "Giant" firms (>$500M raised) had lower layoff percentages (15.7%), their massive scale resulted in over **157,000 total jobs lost**.
* **Capital vs. Survival:** Debunked the "Big Money" myth by identifying unicorns like Quibi ($1.8B) and BlockFi ($1B) that collapsed despite massive funding.
